About Karikku

Karikku Street Food is a dream project of two passionate food lovers. The name Karikku comes from tender coconut – a true symbol of Kerala. From this food truck, we bring you authentic South Indian (Kerala) food along with our signature creations, served fresh and made right in front of you.
